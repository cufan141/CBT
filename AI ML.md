## AI / ML Overview

MindAlign uses several types of AI/ML components:

### NLP Text Classification

- Classifies user text (check-ins, messages, journaling) into risk levels:
  - No risk
  - Mild distress
  - High risk
  - Imminent risk
- Implemented using supervised models such as:
  - Fine-tuned transformer models.
  - Or simpler baselines like logistic regression over text embeddings
